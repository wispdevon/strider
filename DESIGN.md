# Strider Flow Design Notes

## Visual direction
- Calm, editorial workspace aesthetic inspired by Apple and Notion.
- Background uses a muted paper-like tone with a subtle dotted texture.
- UI surfaces are soft, rounded, and lightly elevated with restrained shadows.
- Accent treatment leans into a dark neutral / titanium palette with crisp, premium contrast inspired by Nothing phone design.
- Typography uses a polished system sans stack for a clean, premium feel.

## Color system
- Background: warm off-white neutral.
- Foreground: dark charcoal for strong readability.
- Panels: light neutral surfaces with subtle depth.
- Border: low-contrast, soft separators.
- Accent: dark neutral/titanium for headers, active actions, and emphasis.
- Accent soft: pale neutral tint used for hover and secondary states.

## Interaction language
- Rounded cards and panels with gentle elevation.
- Buttons use restrained shadows and slightly stronger contrast for primary actions.
- Focus states are subtle and intentional rather than flashy.
- The overall feel should remain calm, polished, and understated.
